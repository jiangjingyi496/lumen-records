Now let your servant go in peace,
如今让您的仆人安息吧
Your quiet word has brought release;
您轻柔的话语已带来解脱
For these old eyes have seen the sign
因为这双老眼已见到那征兆
That waited long beyond their time —
它在久远的岁月里等待——
A small light cradled in our night,
在我们的黑夜中怀抱着一束微光
A gentle dawn within our sight.
在我们的视野里迎来了一抹温柔的晨曦

What now I hold is not a word,
此刻我拥有的并非言语
Not shadow, not an echo heard,
非阴影，非耳畔回响
But flesh and face and quiet breath,
而是血肉之躯，面容，轻柔的呼吸
A warmth more lasting than our death.
一种比死亡更持久的温暖

The ancient lamp was never lost,
那盏古灯从未遗失
But kept through every night and frost;
历经无数寒夜霜雪
The ancient promise, whispered low,
那句古老的低语诺言
Wakes within this child I know.
唤醒了我所熟知的这孩子

Light, you came into our night,
光明，你降临我们的黑夜
Light, we kindled lamps so bright.
光明，我们点亮明灯如此明亮
Light, you came to those you own,
光明，你来到你的子民身边
You dwell in flesh, no more alone.
你居于肉身，不再孤单

Now I may go, for I have seen,
如今我可以离去，因为我已得见
Now I may rest, where hope has been;
如今我可以安息，在希望曾驻足之地
A child has walked our mortal way…
一个孩子走过我们凡人的道路……
Light… within these arms… stay…
光明……在这些臂弯里……留下……
Light… within us… stay…
光明……在我们心中……留下……
Light…
光明……
